About ABC Engineering College Tech Spark 2027

ABC Engineering College proudly presents Tech Spark 2027, a national-level technical festival that brings together innovative minds from schools and colleges. The event encourages creativity, teamwork, leadership, and technical excellence through exciting competitions and workshops.

🎯 Our Mission

To inspire students to explore emerging technologies, improve problem-solving skills, and gain practical experience through real-world technical events.

🌟 Our Vision

To become one of the leading technology festivals by creating opportunities for learning, innovation, collaboration, and professional growth.

  • Artificial Intelligence Workshop
  • Ideathon Competition
  • Technical Symposium
  • Industry Expert Sessions
  • Networking Opportunities
  • Certificates & Cash Prizes
