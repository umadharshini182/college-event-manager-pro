Privacy Policy

ABC Engineering College Tech Spark 2027 values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your full name, email address, college name, department, year of study, and event registration details during the registration process.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your information is used to process registrations, manage events, send confirmations, issue certificates, and communicate important event updates.

3. Payment Information

Payment transactions are processed securely. We do not store your debit card, credit card, UPI PIN, or banking credentials on our website.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Email Communication

Participants may receive confirmation emails, event reminders, announcements, and important notifications related to Tech Spark 2027.

6. Third-Party Services

Trusted third-party services may be used for payment processing, email delivery, analytics, or hosting. These providers are expected to protect your information appropriately.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with immediate effect.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the organizing committee using the Contact section available on our website.
